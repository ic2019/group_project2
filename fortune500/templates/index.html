Welcome investors, let's have "A Peek into US Fortune 500 Financial Data".


We are a one stop shop where investors gain the ability to view different variables about their favorite Fortune 500 companies.


While navigating through our website you will be able to find a stock analysis on Fortune 500 companies, revenue and profit statistics for these companies and even the profitability per employee. This allows investors to dive deep into the worker productivity of the Fortune 500 Companies that interest them. Additionally, you will be able to find a sector analysis of the different industries that the Fortune 500 companies consist of. Lastly, our website contains a map of the where the companies are shown based on the amount of revenue they have. Our website gives investors the tools they need to make better and more informed investments while adding to their portfolio.
